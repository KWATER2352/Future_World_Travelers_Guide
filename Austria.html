Austria

Vienna

  • Vienna International Airport (VIE): Taxis from location to city take 20-30 minutes for 35 euros/$39 USD plus 10% tip
  • City Airport Train (CAT): Airport -> Wien Mitte train station for 12 euros/$13.25 USD one way or 21 euros/$23 USD round trip. Children traveling ages 4-10 for free.
  • Wiener Linien: A public transportation system in the cities center costing 2 euros for a single ride.
