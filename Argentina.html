Argentina

  • International Airport: Ministro Pistarini (Ezeiza International EZE)

    • Hub for Argentian airlines, 14 miles (35 minutes, $30 USD/ 1,750 Argentinian pesos) from Buenos Aires.
    No standard fare for taxis, bus-lines from EZE and rental cars available.

  • Domestic Airport: Jorge Newbury Airfield

    • Handles only domestic flights located a mile and a half from downtown Buenos Aires.

  • Ferries: Buquebus and Colonia Express
    Montevido, Uruguay -> Buenos Aires. Crosses the River Plate (Rio de plata).

    • 2,900 - 8,700 pesos/ $50 USD - $150 USD each way. Depending on time of day and whether journey is by boat only or includes a bus transfer. Will contain border and customs.

  • Metro System: Subte

    • Both metro and bus are paid with a Sube card. Available at metro stations, Tourist Assistance Centers, kioks, and corner/tobacco shops.
