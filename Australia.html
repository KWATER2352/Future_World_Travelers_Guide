    • Taxis from airports to downtown take 30 min, $60 Australian/$40 USD.

  • Sky Bus Shuttle runs frequently from airport and city, $19 A/$13 USD
    • More sky bus information

Sydney

  • Sydney Kingsford Smith International Airport (SYD): about 25 minutes from the city in taxi for $50 A/ $34 USD
  • Shuttle options can be arranged at Redy2Go desks.
  • Sydney Airport Link Train departs every 10 minutes with an Opal card. One way fare is $20 A/$13.50 USD for adults.
